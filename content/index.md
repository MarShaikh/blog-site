"A garden of notes — half-finished, half-remembered. Wander; the gate is always open."

I am writing on this website with the idea that I will mirror my Obsidian vault here. Not a 1-1 mirror, rather a cleaner, approachable version of it. Over the past two years, it has grown substantially. What started as a way for me to keep track of my work, quickly became a way for me to do journaling, writing about new things I learn on the job or off the job and just random stuff, even including about my life. 

I contemplated for quite a while about starting my blog, or just a place for me to put all of my filtered, refined thoughts and learnings that I've done at my work or outside my work. And so this is the place where I hope I will be doing just that. The quote at the top of the page, which, for full disclosure, I did not write, it was written by Claude. These notes serve as a way of me documenting my learning and that's it. A lot of the text which I had written in a not-so-neat way has been rewritten for the general web using an LLM. I have not abandoned my own thinking and I will not let an LLM write things for me from scratch (except a quote here and there LMAO). This page has been written using my voice with Wispr Flow.
